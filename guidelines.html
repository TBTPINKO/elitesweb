Our Rules for a Safe Community

To ensure everyone has a safe and enjoyable experience, please follow these guidelines:

1. Respectful Engagement

Please engage in conversations with respect and honor. Do not disrespect, insult, bully, or make fun of others. Treat other members like you wish to be treated.

2. Prohibited Content

Discrimination, hate speech, or harassment is prohibited from our discussions. Keep discussions appropriate for all ages, including no NSFW content, or discussions related to it.

3. Illegal Substances

Discussions of illegal substances such as cocaine or meth are strictly prohibited.

4. Spamming

Avoid spamming, as our team considers it annoying. Spamming server ads or self-promo links are also prohibited.

5. Appropriate Channel Usage

Use appropriate discussions for the appropriate channels, and avoid off-topic discussions in the server.

6. Personal Information

Do not leak our fans' personal information without their consent, including age, name, pronouns, etc.

7. Language and Content

NSFW content is strictly prohibited from our daily discussions. This is mainly an English server, so we prohibit the use of other languages in our general chat.

8. Alt Accounts & Ban Evasion

Joining with a secondary or alt account is allowed. However, ensure that your main account isn’t kicked or banned. Ban evasion is strictly prohibited within the server.

9. Discord's Terms of Service

Following Discord’s terms of service and guidelines is a must in our community.
